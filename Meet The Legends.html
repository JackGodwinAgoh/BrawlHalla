Artemis-The Awnser

Born Mary Washington in West Plaza, New York, she was a voracious reader with a love for strawberry ice cream. While Mary had a relatively normal childhood, her parents sometimes wondered why she would read stories of the goddess Artemis killing the hunter Orion over and over again. One day, she found herself in a dark alley, confronted by a creature from the void of space. The creature infected her, and she was transformed into Artemis, a being of cosmic power. Now, as The Destroyer, she seeks to eliminate Orion, whom she perceives as a threat to the universe

Orion-The Mysterious Stranger

Orion is an enigmatic warrior clad in futuristic armor crafted by Asgardian dwarves. He arrived in Valhalla as a comet from space, and though he initially appeared aloof, he has become one of Valhalla's greatest and most popular champions. Orion does not speak of his past, and his true origins remain a mystery. If you have further questions or need more details about these legends, feel free to ask!

Dusk-The Renegade Sorcerer

Dusk was once a powerful sorcerer who sought forbidden magic in hopes of gaining more power. His obsession led him to use dark, destructive magic, which transformed him into a shadowy being made of ash. Now known as The Ashen Nightmare, he uses shadow magic to summon minions to fight for him. While he's a fearsome figure, Dusk is haunted by his past and is on a quest for redemption, hoping to undo the curse he brought upon himself..

Vector-The Cybernetic Hunter

Vector hails from a future where humanity has merged with technology. After surviving a life-threatening battle, he was transformed into a cybernetic warrior through extensive enhancements, improving his strength, speed, and combat skills. His cybernetic body makes him nearly unstoppable, with a built-in arsenal of advanced weaponry. Now, as a relentless hunter, he travels across time and space, eliminating any threats to the future of his people. Despite his mechanical enhancements, his human will and determination remain his strongest asset.

Koji-The Wanderer

Koji comes from a family of renowned demon hunters in feudal Japan. His ancestors were celebrated for their ability to track and eliminate supernatural threats. Throughout his life, Koji trained relentlessly to follow in the footsteps of his ancestors. He eventually faced off against the demon Kagima, a powerful and ruthless creature that had terrorized the land. Koji's battle with Kagima was a legendary one, and he emerged victorious, severing the demon's head in an epic confrontation. As a result, Koji earned the title of “The Demon Hunter” and has since become a symbol of courage and strength, always ready to defend the world from dark forces.

Jiro-The Shogun's Shadow

Jiro is a deadly shinobi from feudal Japan, a master of both shadow manipulation and assassination. His unique ability allows him to control and steal the shadows of others, turning them into extensions of his will. With this power, he can strike from the shadows, vanishing and reappearing with deadly precision. Jiro is known for his cunning and stealth, making him an unmatched assassin. He is feared for his ability to blend into the darkness, ensuring that his enemies never see him coming. His quiet and deadly nature makes him a formidable adversary in battle.

Wu-Shang The Traveler

Wu Shang’s lore is inspired by Chinese folklore and martial traditions. He embodies the spirit of legendary warriors and mythical figures from Chinese history. Wu Shang's name, "Qiang," refers to the traditional Chinese spear, symbolizing strength, precision, and resilience. His weaponry, including the spear and his martial arts skills, reflect his rich cultural heritage. Wu Shang is portrayed as an immortal-like hero, a figure who represents strength and unyielding will. Drawing inspiration from Taoist immortals and heroic generals, Wu Shang’s resilience and fortitude make him an unbreakable force in combat. His mastery of combat and his steadfast nature make him a warrior who will never be defeated.

Hattori The Demon Bride

Hattori is a highly skilled ninja who hails from feudal Japan. Trained under the legendary ninja master Hattori Hanzo, she has mastered the art of ninjutsu. Her training focuses on stealth, speed, and precision, making her a deadly opponent. Hattori’s skills allow her to move unseen and strike swiftly, vanishing before her enemies even realize what has happened. She is known for her agility and combat prowess, and her devotion to her training makes her an unyielding warrior. Hattori’s dedication to the shadowy ways of the ninja makes her a formidable presence in battle, able to take on even the toughest foes.

Ragnir-The Dragon

Ragnir is a fearsome and rugged warrior with a deep history rooted in the art of dragon hunting. Hailing from a world where dragons once ruled, Ragnir has dedicated his life to hunting these powerful creatures, using his immense strength and cunning to track and slay them. He wields a mighty axe that has been passed down through generations of dragon hunters. With his unmatched knowledge of dragonkind and the relentless will to protect his people from these mighty beasts, Ragnir has become a legend.

Yumiko-The Kitsune

Yumiko is a skilled archer and an ancient spirit who has lived for centuries. Hailing from a world steeped in nature and mysticism, she is one with the forest and its creatures. Yumiko’s prowess with a bow is unmatched, and her connection to nature grants her the ability to summon powerful magical forces through her arrows. Her bond with the forest allows her to call upon elemental magic, using it to heal or attack. .

Brynn-The Chooser of the Slain

Brynn is a fierce warrior from a long line of legendary shieldmaidens. Hailing from the frigid northern lands, she is a powerful Valkyrie who commands both the strength of a mighty warrior and the grace of a warrior's heart. Brynn wields her enchanted battle axe, which she uses with unparalleled skill in both ranged and close combat. She also possesses an indomitable will to fight for justice. As a Valkyrie, she has been tasked with guiding fallen heroes to the afterlife, but she also defends the living from threats in the mortal realm. Brynn embodies strength, loyalty, and the honor of a true warrior.
