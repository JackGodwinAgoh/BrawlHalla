About

Brawlhalla is a multiplayer cross-platform game in which you battle other players using characters called legends. There are over 60 legends for you to choose from, each with a unique weapon set and signature moves that make them versatile in their own way. Choose the legend that suits you best and sharpen your skills to climb the ranks to become a Valhallan, which is only attainable if you are in the top 0.28% of your region.
