Tin

Tin 1: 0 - 874 Elo

Tin 2: 875 - 924 Elo

Tin 3: 925 - 974 Elo

Tin rank is the starting point for new players who are just beginning to learn the game’s mechanics. At this stage, players are still getting familiar with basic controls, movement, and attacks. There is little to no strategy involved, with most players relying on button-mashing rather than intentional gameplay. Many struggle with recovery and frequently fall off the stage by accident due to a lack of awareness of jump and dodge mechanics. Attacks are often random and uncoordinated, with little understanding of combos, spacing, or effective dodging.

Bronze

Bronze 1: 975 - 1024 Elo

Bronze 2: 1025 - 1074 Elo

Bronze 3: 1075 - 1124 Elo

At the Bronze rank, players begin to develop a basic understanding of Brawlhalla’s mechanics but still make frequent mistakes. Dodging becomes more common, but many players struggle with timing, making their dodges predictable and ineffective. Some players start experimenting with light attack combos, though execution is often inconsistent. Despite improving slightly, recovery skills remain a weak point, and many still fall off the stage due to poor jump and dodge usage. A large number of players at this level continue to rely on spamming attacks rather than focusing on positioning, spacing, or reading their opponent’s movements.

Silver

Silver 1: 1125 - 1174 Elo

Silver 2: 1175 - 1224 Elo

Silver 3: 1225 - 1274 Elo

Silver-ranked players begin to develop stronger movement skills and start incorporating basic strategies into their gameplay. Light attack combos become more consistent, allowing for better damage output and smoother engagements. Dodging is used more intentionally, though many players still have predictable dodge habits that can be punished by more experienced opponents. Recovery skills improve significantly, with players utilizing wall jumps and dodges more effectively to return to the stage. At this level, the skill gap between low Silver and high Silver players becomes apparent, as higher-ranked players display better positioning, reaction time, and combo execution.

Platinum

Platinum 1: 1475 - 1524 Elo

Platinum 2: 1525 - 1574 Elo

Platinum 3: 1575 - 1624 Elo

Platinum 4: 1625 - 1674 Elo

Platinum-ranked players have a solid grasp of advanced movement techniques such as fast falling, gravity cancels, and directional dodging. At this level, gameplay becomes more strategic, with a strong emphasis on combos and dodge reads. Players can effectively predict their opponents' dodging habits and counter accordingly, leading to more efficient punishes and extended combos. Mind games and baiting tactics are commonly used to trick opponents into making mistakes, creating openings for high-damage follow-ups. Stage control becomes a key focus, with players actively managing space to maintain an advantage, control engagements, and limit their opponent’s movement options. Diamond

2000+ Elo – The Elite Tier

Diamond-ranked players are among the best in Brawlhalla, demonstrating near-complete mastery of the game's mechanics. Their movement, spacing, and advanced combos are executed with precision, allowing them to control engagements and dictate the pace of the match. At this level, players can consistently predict their opponents’ moves, using advanced dodge reads to extend combos and maximize damage. Their decision-making is highly refined, with every action serving a specific purpose—there are no wasted movements or attacks. Mistakes are punished severely, and adaptability is key, as Diamond players can quickly adjust their strategies based on their opponent’s playstyle. This rank is a testament to a player’s dedication, skill, and deep understanding of the game’s competitive dynamics

Valhallan

2100+ Elo + Top 150 in Region – The Best of the Best

The Valhallan rank is the pinnacle of Brawlhalla’s ranked system, reserved for the absolute best players in the world. Those who achieve this rank consistently compete in high-level tournaments and dominate the top leaderboards. Every aspect of their gameplay is perfected, from precise dodge reads to flawlessly punishing even the smallest mistakes. Their mechanics, reactions, and decision-making are unmatched, allowing them to outplay opponents with strategic precision and near-flawless execution. Valhallan players demonstrate an elite level of skill, making split-second decisions that can turn the tide of a match
