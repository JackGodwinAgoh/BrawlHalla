Scythe

The Scythe the first weapon to allow for active inputs when performing Light Attacks, which differentiates the result of an attack depending on what directional key is held down after hitting an opponent. In combination with the weapon's quick grappling attacks, players can easily fall into fast combos under the Scythe's curved blade.For players who enjoy controlling space and using a mix of fast, wide-reaching attacks. It’s great for players who want to keep the enemy off balance

Katars

Katars is one of the Weapons used in the game Brawlhalla. The Katars are set of daggers that rest on the user's knuckles. While small in size, the Katars are proficient in rapid fire attacks, making it a good tool for sharp and snappy combos. Fast, quick melee weapon with emphasis on agile movement,est for players who like to focus on speed, agility, and quick combos. Sword

The Sword is a light, single-handed blade. An agile weapon, Sword wielders will slash away at their opponents with a barrage of quick hitting attacks.Balanced, fast melee weapon with both horizontal and vertical slashes,The Sword has a medium attack range, making it neither too short nor too long. It allows players to strike quickly while keeping a safe distance. Its speed makes it great for chaining attacks together into devastating combos. Rocket Lance

The Rocket Lance is a polearm with a rocket booster at the rear of its hilt. Legends will often use the rocket for mobility purposes as well as attacking. This weapon is very useful for aerial combat.The Rocket Lance is a long-range melee weapon that thrives on aerial mobility and powerful knockbacks. It allows players to rush down opponents with high-speed attacks or control space with long-reaching strikes. With its boosted movement and devastating attacks, the Rocket Lance is great for keeping opponents at a distance while dealing heavy damage. For players who enjoy long-range combat and aerial mobility, offering high damage potential

Hammer/Grapple Hammer

The Grapple Hammer is a large hammer that usually has a blunt side and a pointed side. The "face" of the hammer is used for bashing enemies whereas the "claw" of the hammer is used to grapple them.The Hammer is a high-damage, heavy-hitting melee weapon that excels at knockback and stage control. It’s designed for players who prefer slow but powerful attacks that can KO opponents at low health. The Hammer focuses on grounded dominance with strong, single-hit moves that can easily disrupt an enemy’s momentum.The Hammer is perfect for players who like heavy-hitting, high-damage attacks and controlling the pace of battle. It rewards precise timing and patience, punishing enemies with some of the strongest knockback attacks in the game
